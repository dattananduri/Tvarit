# Tvarit
Tvarit is a hyperlocal request-and-delivery platform designed for small towns, villages, mandals, and semi-urban regions where instant delivery services like Instamart, Blinkit, and Zepto are unavailable.

The goal is to digitally connect:
->Customers
->Local Shops
->Delivery Partners and provide a fast, affordable, and reliable delivery experience.

While tier-1 cities enjoy 10-minute delivery apps, smaller towns lack the infrastructure for dark stores. Tvarit bridges this gap by digitizing the local commerce workflow: turning any local resident with a vehicle into a temporary delivery partner and connecting them with customers who need items fetched from local markets.
